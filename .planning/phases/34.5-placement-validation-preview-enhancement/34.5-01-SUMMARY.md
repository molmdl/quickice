## Decisions Made

- **Single-molecule validation approach:** Validate ONE molecule at a time (O(M) cost) rather than full system preview (O(N×M) cost), avoiding violation of REQUIREMENTS.md computational constraint
- **Read-only operation:** Validation method does not modify MoleculetypeRegistry or insert molecules, enabling safe preview before commit
- **Reuse existing infrastructure:** Leverage existing _build_existing_atoms_tree() and _check_overlap() methods for consistency and maintainability
- **Structured feedback:** Return PlacementValidationResult dataclass with detailed validation information (bounds check, overlap detection, nearest atom info) for clear user feedback
