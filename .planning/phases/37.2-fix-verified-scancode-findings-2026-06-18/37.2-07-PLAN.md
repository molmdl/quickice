**3. ranking.md — Fix "Rewards" wording (#38):**

At line 11, CHANGE:
```markdown
3. **Diversity Score** - Rewards structural uniqueness via O-O distance fingerprint
```
TO:
```markdown

```

This is more accurate because the diversity score is computed as `1 - mean_similarity`, meaning higher structural similarity → lower diversity score → higher (worse) combined score. The word "Penalizes" correctly conveys that structural similarity hurts the score, while "Rewards" implied the opposite.
