     - Create scalar array: `vtkFloatArray()` with name "PropertyRanking"

     - Add to molecule: `molecule.GetAtomData().AddArray(scalar_array)`
     - Enable scalar coloring:
       ```python
       self._mapper.ScalarVisibilityOn()

       self._mapper.SetLookupTable(ctf)
       ```
