- **comb-rule 1 (sigma/epsilon) instead of comb-rule 2 (C6/C12):** Column headers already said "sigma (nm)" and "epsilon (kJ/mol)", all other atomtypes (NA, CL, c3, os) were already in sigma/epsilon format. Switching to comb-rule 1 makes the format self-consistent and eliminates ambiguity.
- **Module-level constants instead of hardcoded f-strings:** TIP4P_ICE_OW_SIGMA and TIP4P_ICE_OW_EPSILON provide single source of truth, preventing future copy-paste exponent errors across the 6 functions.
