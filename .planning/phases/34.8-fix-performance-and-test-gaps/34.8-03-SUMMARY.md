## Decisions Made
- Used O-O distance distribution histogram (n_bins=20) as structural fingerprint rather than more complex approaches like radial distribution functions or bond angle distributions — simpler, sufficient for ranking discrimination
- Cosine similarity for histogram comparison — standard metric for probability distribution comparison, range [0,1] maps naturally to diversity
- Diversity = 1 - mean_similarity convention — higher diversity = more unique, consistent with existing "higher = better" convention for diversity_score
- Neutral score 0.5 for degenerate cases (single candidate, no O-O pairs) — avoids crash, signals uncertainty without biasing ranking
