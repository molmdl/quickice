## Decisions Made
- **count=3 in ice_molecule_index:** MW is computed AFTER wrapping from O, H1, H2 positions, so MW is not included in the molecule_index for wrapping (distinct from WATER_ATOMS_PER_MOLECULE=4)
- **fudgeLJ=0.5 safe for all contexts:** For TIP4P-ICE water-only systems, fudgeLJ value is irrelevant (no 1-4 pairs), making 0.5 safe. For GAFF2 systems (THF, CH4, custom molecules), 0.5/0.8333 is the correct Amber standard.
- **Regression test design:** BUG-05 test verifies HW1 Z ≠ HW2 Z for molecules with different H positions (before fix, they would be identical); MW-01 test verifies MW position matches compute_mw_position() from wrapped O/H1/H2; DEFLT-01 test parses [ defaults ] section from all 6 TOP writers
