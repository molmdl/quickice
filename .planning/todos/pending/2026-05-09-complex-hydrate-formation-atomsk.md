## Problem

Current hydrate generation relies on GenIce2 which handles simple clathrate hydrates (sI, sII, sH). Complex hydrate structures (e.g., filled ice, semi-clathrates, mixed phases) may require alternative approaches.

## Solution

Research and potentially adapt atomsk methodology for complex hydrate formation:
- Atomsk: https://atomsk.univ-lille.fr/
- Investigate if atomsk can generate complex hydrate templates
- Evaluate integration feasibility with QuickIce workflow
- Consider: adaptation vs. external tool pipeline

Target: Post-v6 milestone (far future work)
Risk: May be out of scope for QuickIce's design philosophy
